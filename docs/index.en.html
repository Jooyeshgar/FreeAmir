Features

Amir's core capabilities

Amir offers a comprehensive set of financial, inventory, HR, and tax tools in a single open-source platform.

Double-entry accounting

Accurate transaction recording with debit/credit principles and automatic document balancing.

Journal entries & general ledger

View and manage all financial entries in the general ledger and journals.

Financial reports

Balance sheet, profit & loss, trial balance, and management reports.

Customers & suppliers

Manage customer and supplier information and history.

Sales & purchase invoices

Issue and manage sales and purchase invoices with full details.

Sales & purchase returns

Record returns with correct impact on inventory and accounts.

Warehouse management

Create and manage multiple warehouses and transfer goods between them.

Stock tracking

Real-time inventory visibility and goods movement tracking.

Weighted average costing

Automatic cost of goods calculation using moving weighted average in a perpetual system.

Employee management

Register and manage employee information.

Attendance

Clock-in/out tracking, daily and monthly work records, and data import from external sources.

Salary & payroll

Monthly salary calculation, deductions, benefits, payslips, and reports.

Moadian integration

Full connection to Iran's Moadian tax system. Each company has its own certificate and private key.

Setup guide ←

Tax reports

Generate reports required for Iranian tax compliance.

Multi-company

Manage multiple companies simultaneously with separate, independent data.

Multiple fiscal years

Create and manage multiple independent fiscal years for each company.

Roles & permissions

Define different roles and manage granular access controls.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is Amir really free?

Yes. Amir is released under the GPL-3 license with no license fees or subscriptions.

Can I host it on my own server?

Yes. Amir is designed for self-hosted deployment. Your data stays fully under your control.

Does it support Moadian?

Yes. Amir supports the Moadian system. Each company can configure its own certificate and private key.

Does it support multiple companies?

Yes. Amir supports multiple companies with independent fiscal years.

Does it support payroll?

Yes. Salary calculation, attendance, payslips, and reports are all included.

Is it production-ready?

Amir is under active development. Evaluate with test data before production use.
