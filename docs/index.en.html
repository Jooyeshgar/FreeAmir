Introduction

Free accounting for the real needs of Iranian businesses

Amir is a free and open-source accounting application built on Laravel. The project focuses on double-entry postings, sales and purchase invoices, inventory management, fiscal-year operations, and a Persian-first user experience.

The project is still under active development and should be evaluated with test data, each organization's needs, and independent accounting controls before production use.

Features

Amir's core capabilities

Accounting and reporting

Income and expense management, invoices and receipts, financial reports, and the foundations required for Iranian tax rules.

Warehousing and goods

Product management, inventory, stock movements, and cost of goods using the perpetual moving weighted-average method.

Fiscal year

Create, maintain, export, and import fiscal-year data while respecting accounting boundaries.

Attendance

Clock-in and clock-out tracking, daily and monthly work tracking, and data import from external sources.

Salary and payroll

Monthly salary calculation, management of deductions and benefits, payslips, and related reports.

Open source and extensible

Free code with Laravel's service-oriented architecture for transparent contribution, review, and development.
